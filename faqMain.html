Reducing Food Waste, One Loop at a Time!

Connecting Restaurants, Grocery Stores, and Communities to give Surplus Food a Second Life

Who Are We?

FoodLoop is a Louisiana based organization dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting restaurants, grocery stores, and local food businesses with community members and organizations who can use their surplus food.

How It Works
  1. Businesses upload thier surplus foods and available items to our website
  2. Users browse organizations listings and claim what they need/want
  3. User can either pick up the package or request for a volunteer to deliver the package to them
Features

For the conveniece of the users, our site offers:

  1. Real time listings: So you always know what is available
  2. Free to use: Becuase you should never have to pay fo food safety
  3. Eco-Friendly Impact: By rducing waste and carbon foorprint
  4. Business Analytics: You can track your impact over time
